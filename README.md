# 📄 Swift PDF Utility App

A sleek and powerful iOS app built using **Swift**, **PDFKit**, and **VisionKit** that lets you scan, merge, watermark, and manage your PDF files—all locally and securely, with an intuitive UI.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📸 **Scan Documents**  
  Capture physical documents using your iPhone's camera with VisionKit. Detects edges and optimizes scans.

- 🖇️ **Merge PDFs**  
  Easily merge multiple PDFs into one. Select files from your device and rearrange before combining.

- 💧 **Custom Watermarks**  
  Add personalized watermarks to your PDFs. Customize text, color, position, and font style for your branding.

- 📝 **Annotate and Edit**  
  Preview scanned or existing PDFs and annotate them as needed before sharing or saving.

- 🔒 **All Processing is Local**  
  Your files stay on your device—no internet required for any core functionality.

- 📤 **Share Anywhere**  
  Share your final PDFs directly from the app via Mail, Messages, or your preferred storage app.

---

## 🧰 Tech Stack

- **Swift** – Core language
- **PDFKit** – PDF creation, merging, and editing
- **VisionKit** – Document scanning with camera
- **UIKit** – UI implementation

## 📦 Installation

1. Clone the repository
2. Open `.xcodeproj` in Xcode
3. Build and run on iOS Simulator or a real device (Camera required for scanning)
